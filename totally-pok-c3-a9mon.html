Totally Pokémon is a music CD released in North America on January 23, 2001. Most of the songs were recorded by a sextet called Johto. It has also been released in Czech, Dutch, European French, German, Hungarian, Italian, Brazilian Portuguese, Portuguese, Latin American Spanish and European Spanish.

The album is available on Spotify.

Tracklist

Instrumental Bonus Disc

A promotional version of the album released in Australia also included a bonus disc with instrumental versions of the songs. These tracks are exclusive to this edition and are different mixes than the karaoke tracks found on the first disc.
