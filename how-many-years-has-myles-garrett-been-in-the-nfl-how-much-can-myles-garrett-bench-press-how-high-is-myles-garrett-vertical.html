Myles Lorenz Garrett, an American football defensive end with the Cleveland Browns of the National Football League, was born on December 29, 1995.

He played college football at Texas A&M and was chosen first overall by the Browns in the 2017 NFL Draft. In 2016, he was named a unanimous All-American.

Throughout his NFL career, Garrett has earned three Pro Bowl selections and two first-team All-Pro honors.

Garrett played football, basketball, and track as a letter winner at Martin High School in Arlington, Texas.

He won the 2013 Landry Award, presented to the best high school football player in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, for his senior season’s 19.5 sacks.
