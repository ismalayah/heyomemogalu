30 Great Movies Like 'The Princess Diaries', Ranked By Viewers

The Prince and Me spins a tale of unexpected romance between a down-to-earth college student, Paige Morgan, and the Crown Prince of Denmark, who is attending college incognito. Julia Stiles stars as Paige, who falls for the charming and rebellious "Eddie," played by Luke Mably, without knowing his royal secret. Their cross-continental love story unravels challenges of duty, ambition, and the reach for a fairytale ending.

Those fond of The Princess Diaries may find The Prince and Me particularly delightful for its similar theme of ordinary life meeting royal obligations. Both films weave narratives of self-discovery, romance, and the seldom smooth melding of contrasting worlds, all centered around strong female leads carving out their destinies.
