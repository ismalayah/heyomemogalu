Who is Tayshia Adams ex-husband Josh Bourelle?

TAYSHIA Adams’ ex-husband Josh Bourelle was her first boyfriend.

Out of the six years together, the rumored Bachelorette was married for a year. The couple divorced citing “irreconcilable differences.”

Who was Tayshia Adams married to?

Tayshia Adams, the new Bachelorette, was married to Mammoth Lake, CA native Josh Bourelle. 

It is unclear where the pair met but they were married for one year and six months before calling it quits.

Tayshia opened up to Colton Underwood about her marriage during her appearance on season 23 of the Bachelor.

 “This past year and a half has been kind of hard for me and it’s actually because I was married, and I got divorced,” she said.

“I actually married my first boyfriend and I was with him for about six years or so.

“You can’t make someone want to be married,” she said.

Josh still lives in Orange County where he has moved on with a pretty blonde as he juggles his family-owned roofing business.

When was Tayshia on the Bachelor?

Tayshia was on season 23 of the Bachelor starring Colton Underwood.

She became a fan favorite on the show and reached the final two with Hannah Godwin. 

Colton did not select either of them and chased after another contestant, Cassie Randolph.

When was she on Bachelor in Paradise?

Upon Tayshia’s elimination, she got to go on season six of Bachelor in Paradise in 2019 alongside other fan favorites. 

She is rumored to take over Clare Crawley's new season of the Bachelorette.

When did Tayshia date John Paul Jones?

Tayshia and John Paul Jones began dating while in Mexico during Bachelor in Paradise.

There was a lot of speculation about who broke up with who, but apparently it was a mutual decision according to the pair.

After their breakup, Tayshia went to visit John in Maryland to try and mend things.

Rumors swirled around that John relocated to Los Angeles to be near Tayshia, who resided in Newport Beach, California. 

In an episode of the Bachelor Happy Hour podcast, Tayshia told host Rachel Lindsay and guest co-host Becca Kufrin that John moved to California by his own doing. 

“He moved here because he wanted to be an actor and because he was getting auditions and it was just easier for him to attend those as opposed to flying out. 

“That’s why he moved here,” she said. 

Ultimately, their breakup was addressed on her Instagram six weeks after the Bachelor in Paradise reunion episode. 

“I know you guys have been asking about John and I recently, and as much as I like to keep my personal life private, I realize you have all watched and been a part of our journey from the beginning. 

“Therefore, to stay true to that, I need to be open and honest with you in order to heal and move on.

“John and I had something incredibly special. We fell for each other in Paradise, and everything was a whirlwind after that. 

“When the show ended, I truly went back to Maryland to see if what we had was something we could build on without cameras, a production crew, and without feeling any pressure: to simply figure things out on our own terms. 

“We are the best of friends and will continue to remain in each other’s life because regardless of the situation, we make each other happy,” she concluded.
