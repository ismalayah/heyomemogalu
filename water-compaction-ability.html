Water Compaction (Japanese: みずがため Water Fortifying) is an Ability introduced in Generation VII. It is the signature Ability of Sandygast and Palossand.

Effect

In battle

When a Pokémon with Water Compaction is hit by a Water-type move, its Defense rises by two stages. No immunity is granted to Water-type moves, however.

If a Pokémon with this Ability is hit by a Water-type multi-strike move, each hit activates this Ability.

Outside of battle

Water Compaction has no effect outside of battle.
