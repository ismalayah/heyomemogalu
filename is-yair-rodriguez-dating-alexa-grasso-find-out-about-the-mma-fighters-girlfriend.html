Mixed martial artist and Ultimate Fighting Championship athlete caught the media’s attention with suspicion of dating another fellow athlete Alexa Grasso.

Yair Rodríguez Parents And Ethnicity

Yair Rodriguez was born to parents Rigoberto Rodriguez and Norma Alicia Portillo belonging to Mexican ethnicity. The 29 year old was born on October 6, 1992 in Parral, Chihuahua Mexico where he was raised and learned Taekwondo.

His parents brought up Rodriguez with siblings, brother, and sister, and his family supported him in his career of choice as he learned Taekwondo at the age of five.

Although his biological siblings are not involved in sports, he has a cousin named Misaawl Rodriguez, an Olympic boxer.

His professional carer started as a mixed martial artist on October 10, 2011, where he fought at Mexican Fighter Promotions 8 against Jonatan Guzman and won.

Later he competed and won the Ultimate Fighter in May of 2014 leading to his official debut in UFC in 2014 on November 15.

The featherweight athlete has made his parents and family proud with outstanding achievements throughout his career winning the title of The Ultimate Fighter in Latin America Tournament along with Knockout of the Year in MMA Fighting and Combat Press, and many more.
